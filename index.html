Application Information

Application Procedure

  • Step 1: Choose Your Program – Explore our wide range of programs and select the one that aligns with your goals.
  • Step 2: Prepare Your Documents – Gather necessary documents like transcripts, recommendations, and personal statements.
  • Step 3: Complete the Online Application – Fill out the online form with the required information.
  • Step 4: Pay the Application Fee – Submit the non-refundable application fee online.
  • Step 5: Submit Application – Review and submit your application. You'll receive a confirmation email.
  • Step 6: Track Your Application – Monitor the status of your application through our portal.

Application Requirements

  • Academic Transcripts – Official transcripts from all previously attended institutions.
  • Letters of Recommendation – Two academic or professional letters of recommendation.
  • Personal Statement – A 500-700 word essay about your goals and reasons for choosing the program.
  • Standardized Test Scores – GRE, GMAT, SAT, or ACT scores (if applicable).
  • English Proficiency – TOEFL or IELTS scores for international applicants.

Application Deadlines

  • Fall Semester:
    • Early Deadline: April 1
    • Regular Deadline: June 1
  • Spring Semester:
    • Regular Deadline: November 1
  • Late Applications: Late applications may be considered on a case-by-case basis.
